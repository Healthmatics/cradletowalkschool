About Cradle to Walk School

We are dedicated to providing exceptional early childhood education in a nurturing and stimulating environment

Who We Are

Cradle to Walk School is a premier early childhood education center located in Amrahia. We specialize in providing quality education and care for children from infancy through kindergarten age.

Our experienced team of educators and supervisors create a safe, loving, and stimulating environment where children can explore, learn, and grow at their own pace.

Quality Education

Loving Care

Our Vision

To be the leading early childhood education center that nurtures confident, creative, and capable young learners ready for their educational journey.

Our Mission

To provide quality early childhood education through play-based learning, fostering social, emotional, cognitive, and physical development in a safe environment.

Our Values

Love, respect, integrity, excellence, and inclusivity guide everything we do. We believe every child is unique and deserves individualized attention.

Our Programs

Comprehensive early childhood education programs designed for different age groups and developmental stages

Creche

Ages: 6 months - 18 months

Gentle care and early stimulation for our youngest learners. Focus on sensory development, basic motor skills, and establishing routines in a loving environment.

  • • Personalized feeding schedules
  • • Sensory play activities
  • • Safe sleep environments
  • • Early language exposure

Nursery 1

Ages: 18 months - 2.5 years

Introduction to structured learning through play. Development of social skills, basic communication, and independence in a supportive setting.

  • • Potty training support
  • • Basic vocabulary building
  • • Social interaction skills
  • • Creative arts and crafts

Nursery 2

Ages: 2.5 - 3.5 years

Enhanced learning experiences with focus on language development, pre-math concepts, and social-emotional growth through guided activities.

  • • Pre-literacy activities
  • • Number recognition
  • • Group play and sharing
  • • Fine motor development

KG1

Ages: 3.5 - 4.5 years

Structured pre-school curriculum preparing children for formal education. Focus on alphabet recognition, basic math, and school readiness skills.

  • • Alphabet and phonics
  • • Basic counting and shapes
  • • Following instructions
  • • Science exploration

KG2

Ages: 4.5 - 6 years

Advanced kindergarten program ensuring complete school readiness. Comprehensive curriculum covering reading, writing, mathematics, and critical thinking skills.

  • • Reading and writing
  • • Advanced mathematics
  • • Problem-solving skills
  • • Primary school preparation
